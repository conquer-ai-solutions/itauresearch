Data Architecture
Three-Tier Data Model
Tier 1 · Internal
Itaú's Own Data
Historical research reports & pitchbooks — years of analyst work
Salesforce workflow data & CRM records
Internal analyst notes, models, sector coverage
Past presentations and sector thematic pieces
Highest value, lowest friction — already inside the bank
Tier 2 · Licensed Market Data
Bloomberg · Capital IQ · Economatica
Analysts already export data into Excel/PDF as part of their workflow
We ingest those exports — no API violation, no licensing breach
Structured financials, comp tables, consensus estimates
Public company coverage for listed entities
Proven pattern in IB — analyst-in-the-loop ingestion
Tier 3 · Public & Alternative
Open Sources · Agentic Search
SEC EDGAR / CVM (Brazilian SEC) — free API access
Company IR pages, annual reports, press releases
Industry associations, government datasets, news
Niche sector data (IBGE, CONAB, ANP)
Critical for private companies — where AI adds most value
The Hard Problem
Private Company Research
Why This Is Different
No single data provider
Private companies aren't on Bloomberg or Capital IQ. Data is scattered across government databases, industry associations, and niche Brazilian sources.
Unstructured & multilingual
PDF reports from IBGE, CONAB filings, state-level agricultural data, news in Portuguese — none structured for machine consumption.
Sector-specific knowledge
Building a thesis for orange production in Rio Grande do Sul requires understanding that specific market — not just financial models.
How Claude Solves This
Reasoning over sparse data
Claude Opus 4.6 synthesises across fragmentary sources — 5 partial documents become a coherent thesis
1M token context window
No RAG needed. Load all available documents directly. Claude sees everything at once — no retrieval gaps
Analyst-in-the-loop
Analyst uploads whatever they have. Claude reasons, surfaces conflicts, builds the narrative iteratively
Source traceability
Every claim links back to its source document — proven pattern from our Pitchbook POC

End-to-End Flow
Research → Thesis → Presentation
1
Data Ingestion
Internal documents, analyst exports from Bloomberg/Capital IQ, public filings (EDGAR, CVM), agentic web search for alternative data. Three tiers feeding into a unified context.
2
AI-Powered Research
Claude Opus 4.6 synthesises across all sources within its 1M token context window. Builds investment thesis iteratively with the analyst. Proactively surfaces conflicting data and risks.
3
Narrative Builder
Structured outline with full source traceability. Every claim linked to a DocumentReference (document ID, relevance classification, excerpt). Analyst can drag, reorder, edit, and refine.
4
Presentation Generation
IB-grade PPTX following Itaú BBA design standards. Already proven with Pitchbook POC — 13-slide deck generated in ~15 minutes. SSE streaming shows real-time build progress.
